Masters of Arts Club

By Christina Koutsaliari

Get Inspired.

This club was inspired by Mrs. Christina Koutsaliari after devoting her life in the arts and crafts, such as painting, sculpture, literature, music, performing and cinema.

It was her decision to continue and bring these ideas to her hometown of Thessaloniki.

Come join us!

Meetings are on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 18:00 p.m.
